Stop The Cruise

Cruise companies DUMP wastewater into our oceans and SPEW climate-warming emissions into our atmosphere. They do not care about the delicate balance of some of the worlds most sensitive ecosystems. They care about their profits.

The EPA has allowed cruise companies to pollute our oceans and drive the climate crisis for too long. But YOU have a chance to help change that. Please do not wait another moment!

We need 1,938 more signatures by midnight tonight! Demand the EPA regulate cruise companies before it is too late!

Lowe’s and Home Depot continue to sell glyphosate -- a.k.a. the active ingredient in Bayer-Monsanto’s Roundup® -- putting millions at risk from this toxic pesticide. Will you demand Lowe's and Home Depot put the planet before Big Ag profits?

Demand Lowe’s and Home Depot remove cancer-causing Roundup® from store shelves!

Plastic Crisis

Plastic has already made its way into every aspect of life on Earth -- our food, our water, and our air.

Our oceans are being decimated by plastic waste, and the plastic that doesn’t end up in the sea gets poured into landfills and incinerators, seeping into our soil and air. Animals are choking and dying from plastic, while humans unknowingly consume a credit card’s worth of plastic every week.

Powerful plastic corporations and their friends in the fossil fuel industry are raking in massive profits while people, animals, and the planet suffer the devastating consequences of rampant plastic pollution.

Save our public lands

Fossil fuel destruction threatens vast glaciers in the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge, fragile arches in Utah, and even Colorado’s Dinosaur National Monument. And it wrecks wildlife habitat, hurting animals from polar bears to gray wolves to sage grouse.

What’s more – we’re currently in a race against the clock to fight climate change. Scientists have already declared climate change a “code red for humanity.” Fossil fuel development on our public lands and waters has no place in a livable future on this planet.

Stop Factory Farming

Factory farming is one of the worst perpetrators of animal cruelty, air and water pollution and public health harm. But Big Meat and Dairy corporations spend millions lobbying each year to keep you from realizing just how dangerous they really are.

We’re fighting back, but we can’t do it without support from environmental activists like you – and your name is still missing from our petition. Please, with so much on the line, add your signature now to demand the United States Department of Agriculture stop supporting factory farming.

PROTECTION BEES

Bees and other pollinators are responsible for one in every three bites of food we eat. They’re critical to the health and survival of the planet and our food system. And yet they’re facing massive threats from toxic pesticides produced by corporations like Bayer-Monsanto. We need the Environmental Protection Agency to take action NOW, before the destruction of our pollinators is irreversible! Add your name to demand the EPA take action to protect bees from lethal pesticides!
